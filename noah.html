Favourite Cars I Have Owned:

2006 Dodge Caravan

  • 3.3L V6 Motor
  • 4 Spd Automatic Transmission
  • Can Seat 7 people
  • Manufacture Location: Windsor, Ontario
  • Paid: $1300

i bought this van in september 2023 with 311k km, i sold it in july 2025 with 345k km.
it was one of the best vehicles i have owned. its utility let me do a lot of things i
couldnt have done with a normal car. i did more "truck stuff" with this van than most
do with their actual trucks.

some issues i had where rust, a failing door lock actuator, and some leaking power steering hoses.

Fun Story:

a fun thing is that this van was manufactured in windsor, ontario, which is where i was born and
and lived for a while. i have seen the (outside of) the factory, and i have family members who worked
worked in that factory on the assembly lines building these vans.

1999 toyota corolla

  • 1.8L 4-Cyl Motor
  • 4 Spd Automatic Transmission
  • can sit up to 5 people
  • Manufacture Location: Cambridge, Ontario
  • Paid: $900

I bought this car in jan 2020 at 290k km, i sold it in september 2023 with 340k km.
very reliable car, never failed to start. i got it for cheap because it was very worn out
and it needed a lot of work, but it still ran ok. i learned a lot about auto repair from
working on this car. the guy i sold it to, bought it for his daughter to drive, and it was still
in late 2024.

Fun Story:

Due to a small manufacturing defect in the motor during manufacturing, these motors (1st gen 1ZZ-FE) where
VERY prone to burning tons of oil, especially if you skipped oil changes or the motor had high milage.
i used to always carry some extra oil, and check the oil every time i got gas. i would need to top up the
oil about a Liter every 2-3 times i got gas because it burned so much!

2003 Hyundai Elantra

  • 1.6L 4-Cyl Motor
  • 4 Spd Transmission
  • can sit up to 5 people
  • Manufacture Location: Ulsan, South Korea
  • Paid: $2000

bought this car in the summer of 2019 at 170k km, my second car i owned. it was a nice cute little car. it played a little
when you started the car. this was the first car that i had really worked on, and the first car i installed a stereo in.
in all honestly it was a all around average but comfortable and familar car. i sold it in jan 2020 with 185km because i thought the
the transmission was starting to go on it.

Fun Story:

when i bought this car, i was living in edmonton and driving back to red deer every weekend for 2 months, then i moved back to
red deer, then spent every weekend driving up to stony plain from red deer, so i put a ton of miles on this car.

i had only all-seasons on the car, and one friday night in november 2019 i was driving to stony plain and got stuck in a really bad
snow storm, and couldnt see 3 feet past my hood. i was going to turn around but didnt want to get stuck on a off ramp or overpass,
so i kept driving all the way to stony plain, doing 60 or 70 km/ph the whole way.
